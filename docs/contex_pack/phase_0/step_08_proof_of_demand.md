6) INTERVIEW SCRIPT (15 MIN — BEHAVIOR FIRST)

Part 1 — Recent Reality (no hypotheticals)
	1.	“How many intros did you make in the last 2 weeks?”
	2.	“Pick the most recent one—what happened after?”
	3.	“Did you ever have to ask: ‘Did you two connect?’”
	4.	“How often do outcomes disappear?”

Part 2 — Cost (make it painful + real)
	5.	“How much time do you spend following up weekly?”
	6.	“Worst case when outcomes disappear?” (reputation, deal loss, awkwardness)

Part 3 — Workarounds (gold)
	7.	“What do you do today to track intros?”
	8.	“Why does it fail?”

Part 4 — Wedge test (one sentence only)
	9.	“If you could drop a simple receipt link into any intro so outcomes don’t disappear… would you use it?”

Part 5 — Behavioral close (ONLY thing that matters)
	10.	“Can we try it on your next intro this week?”
	11.	“If yes, introduce me to 2 others like you.”
